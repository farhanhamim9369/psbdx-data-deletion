PSBDx Data Deletion Instructions

If you have used Facebook Login to sign in to PSBDx and wish to delete your account or any associated data, please follow the instructions below to ensure complete removal of your personal data.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to psbdx.bd@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request".
  2. Include your registered account email address and/or phone number for verification.
  3. Alternatively, you can submit a deletion request online by visiting this link: Account Deletion Request Page.
  4. Your request will be processed within 7 business days.
  5. Once completed, you will receive a confirmation email.

Additional Support

If you have any questions or face issues with the deletion process, please contact us at psbdx.bd@gmail.com.
